# LOC028_MORNING_ROOM

Pixel art, VGA 256-colour palette, 480x320 pixels (displayed at 240x160), style of early 1990s point-and-click adventure games such as Police Quest or King's Quest VI. Hard pixel edges throughout, no anti-aliasing, no modern rendering techniques. Muted period-appropriate colour palette. Static background scene, no characters, no UI elements, no text overlays.

Morning room with artistic and musical focus. The focal point is an upright piano with dark wood case against the far wall—rendered prominently with keyboard suggested at base. The second anchor is a gramophone on a small table, rendered as a distinctive horn speaker shape with mechanical box base. A chintz sofa occupies the foreground, rendered with patterned fabric suggested through subtle cross-hatching in muted florals. An occasional table holds magazines and books. Watercolour bird pictures hang on the walls—rendered as small framed rectangles. Windows suggest pale daylight filtering through, giving the room a gentle illumination. Walls are cream or pale blue; the atmosphere is refined and comfortable. Colour palette: dark wood piano, pale gramophone horn, muted florals, cream walls, brass accents. Peaceful, artistic atmosphere. Soft interior daylight. No figures.
