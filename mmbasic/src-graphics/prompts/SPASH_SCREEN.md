# SPLASH_SCREEN

Pixel art, VGA 256-colour palette, 640x480 pixels (displayed at 320x240), style of early 1990s point-and-click adventure games such as Police Quest or King's Quest VI. Hard pixel edges throughout, no anti-aliasing, no modern rendering techniques. Muted period-appropriate colour palette. This is a title/splash screen, not a gameplay location.

Darnley Manor is seen across the frozen West Lawn on a grey winter afternoon, a small red-brick Georgian country house rendered in three-quarter perspective so both the front facade and one side elevation are visible — sash windows in ordered rows, a stone portico framing the front door, two chimney stacks trailing thin smoke into a heavy overcast sky. The house sits in the middle distance at eye level; the camera angle is fixed by a low clipped hedge running along the immediate foreground, its snow-capped top crossing the bottom third of the frame, with the bare silhouettes of the young copper beeches from the lawn rising at the left and right edges to frame the composition. Snow covers the lawn between hedge and house, broken only by a single line of footprints leading toward the portico steps. One upstairs window, off to the side of the facade, shows a warm lamp-lit glow against the cold exterior — the only warm colour in an otherwise cold palette — hinting at the drama within. The sky fills the upper third of the image, pale and lightly textured with a suggestion of low winter cloud.

Title text is rendered as part of the scene, not a separate overlay: bold blocky pixel-art lettering reading "THE SEALED ROOM MURDER" spans the upper portion of the image against the sky, in a dark serif-inspired pixel font with hard, unaliased edges matching the rest of the artwork, coloured a deep dried-blood red with a thin black pixel outline for legibility against the pale cloud. Beneath it, smaller and centred, a subtitle line reads "A Darnley Manor Mystery" in a muted grey-white pixel font, same hard-edged treatment, no anti-aliasing on any character.

Colour palette: dark red brick, cream and grey snow, muted grey-blue overcast sky, black window mullions, one small warm amber glow in a single window, deep red and pale grey-white for the title text. Cold, still, slightly foreboding mood — the calm exterior of a house that has just had a murder committed inside it. Flat winter daylight. No figures.
