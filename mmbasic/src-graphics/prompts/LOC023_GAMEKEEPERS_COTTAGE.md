# LOC023_GAMEKEEPERS_COTTAGE

Pixel art, VGA 256-colour palette, 480x320 pixels (displayed at 240x160), style of early 1990s point-and-click adventure games such as Police Quest or King's Quest VI. Hard pixel edges throughout, no anti-aliasing, no modern rendering techniques. Muted period-appropriate colour palette. Static background scene, no UI elements, no text overlays.

Interior of a gamekeeper's cottage, a single modest room lit by a dim fire, a paraffin lamp, and whatever grey daylight filters through a small window. The focal point is an oil-cloth table in the centre of the room, worn but reasonably tidy, at which Ronald Mellors sits on a rough wooden chair - a lean, wiry man in his thirties with black curly hair and strong, good-looking features, wearing working clothes, rendered with the same restrained pixel-art proportions as the room's furniture, seated with forearms resting on the table, facing slightly toward the viewer with an air of settled hostility that does nothing to diminish his rough-edged good looks. On the table near his hands stand a dark glass beer bottle and a small dish holding a single cigarette end. No other figures are present in the scene. Set within the stone hearth behind him is a small black cast iron stove, its flue pipe rising up into the chimney breast; a squat kettle sits on top of the stove, rendered as a simple rounded dark shape with a curved handle and spout. The hearth surround is swept and orderly. Two shotguns are mounted on the wall above the hearth, rendered as dark angular shapes with pale wood stocks. A small chest of drawers stands against one wall, and on top of it sits a lit paraffin lamp, rendered with a pale glass reservoir and a soft warm halo of light spilling from it onto the surrounding wall and floor, supplementing the stove's glow. Two small framed pictures hang on the walls elsewhere in the room, rendered as modest rectangular frames with minimal interior detail - do not add any further pictures beyond these two. A slop-stone sink stands against one wall, plain but clean, with a short section of wooden draining board attached to one side, on which rest a single plate and a mug. A neatly made bed occupies a corner, blankets straightened. The walls are bare plaster or exposed stone, plain but not grimy; the floor is swept flagstone. Colour palette: dull black iron stove, dull orange glow from its grate, warm yellow lamp-light, dark stone and plaster, muted browns and greys, dark wood furniture, pale linens, dark green-brown beer bottle glass. Dim, warmly lit interior in places, otherwise quiet and shadowed. Isolated, slightly guarded atmosphere.
