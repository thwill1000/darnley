# LOC008_HALL

Pixel art, VGA 256-colour palette, 480x320 pixels (displayed at 240x160), style of early 1990s point-and-click adventure games such as Police Quest or King's Quest VI. Hard pixel edges throughout, no anti-aliasing, no modern rendering techniques. Muted period-appropriate colour palette. Static background scene, no characters, no UI elements, no text overlays.

Grand entrance hall with high ceiling and marble floor. The dominant focal point is a broad oak staircase ascending to the right-rear, rendered with bold geometric steps and dark wood railings—perspective emphasizes recession. Two suits of armour stand sentinel on either side of the staircase base, rendered as dark metal silhouettes with visible breastplate reflections. The floor is black-and-white marble in a simple geometric pattern. A heavy oak front door with brass fittings occupies the left foreground, with a damp coir doormat bearing faint muddy impressions. A modest display case is visible on the wall. The ceiling is high and dark, suggested by minimal cross-hatching. Walls are cream with shadows indicating wood panelling. Colour palette: marble blacks and creams, dark wood browns, brass highlights, pale cream walls. No figures. Dim interior lighting, cool daylight from the fanlight above the door.
