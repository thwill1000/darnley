# LOC013_LOUNGE

Pixel art, VGA 256-colour palette, 480x320 pixels (displayed at 240x160), style of early 1990s point-and-click adventure games such as Police Quest or King's Quest VI. Hard pixel edges throughout, no anti-aliasing, no modern rendering techniques. Muted period-appropriate colour palette. Static background scene, no characters, no UI elements, no text overlays.

Interior lounge dominated by comfortable furnishings. The focal point is a three-piece suite arranged on a Chinese carpet, rendered with bold fabric texture suggested through subtle cross-hatching. The suite sits centre-left with cushioned forms clearly readable. The carpet beneath shows geometric patterns in muted burgundy and cream. The fireplace occupies the background wall, rendered as a dark marble structure with a brass mantelpiece—simplified to a horizontal rectangle. Above the mantel, framed portraits are suggested as small rectangular shapes. A side table with magazines and novel stacks visible. Walls are cream with subtle shadow lines suggesting wood panelling below. A door frame in the background. Colour palette: burgundy, cream, dark browns, pale walls. Warm interior lighting, dim. Comfortable, formal atmosphere. No figures.
