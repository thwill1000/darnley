# LOC009_KITCHEN

Pixel art, VGA 256-colour palette, 480x320 pixels (displayed at 240x160), style of early 1990s point-and-click adventure games such as Police Quest or King's Quest VI. Hard pixel edges throughout, no anti-aliasing, no modern rendering techniques. Muted period-appropriate colour palette. Static background scene, no characters, no UI elements, no text overlays.

Kitchen interior with utilitarian fixtures. The focal point is a large ceramic sink with draining board, rendered in cream-white with dark shadows suggesting depth. On the board lies a knife rendered as a dark angular shape. The second anchor is a line of boots by the outside door—simple dark shapes arranged against the wall, some mud-stained. Game birds hang from the ceiling as dark silhouettes with subtle outlines suggesting wings. A wooden preparation table is visible in the mid-ground, rendered with horizontal lines to suggest worn surface. An outside door frame with dark wood is visible on the right. Walls are off-white plaster; ceiling shows wooden beams in dark brown. Colour palette: creams, dark browns, greys, with touches of rust from iron and earth tones from soil. Warm interior lighting, slightly dim. No figures.
