# LOC017_DRIVE

Pixel art, VGA 256-colour palette, 480x320 pixels (displayed at 240x160), style of early 1990s point-and-click adventure games such as Police Quest or King's Quest VI. Hard pixel edges throughout, no anti-aliasing, no modern rendering techniques. Muted period-appropriate colour palette. Static background scene, no characters, no UI elements, no text overlays.

Main drive approach to the house. The focal point is the sweeping drive rendered as two converging light grey gravel lines with snow banks on either side, leading toward stone lions and grand steps framing the house entrance in the background. The lions are rendered as dark silhouettes sitting symmetrically on plinths. The house facade is simplified to essential dark masses. Stone steps climb toward the entrance door. Footprints and tracks in the snow crisscross the drive in multiple directions—boot marks, slipper prints, and shoe tracks suggested through shadowed lines. Wrought-iron gates are visible at the far perspective. Snow patches show melting and compression in high-traffic areas. Colour palette: pale grey gravel, cream snow, dark stone and ironwork, muted sky. Winter daylight, cool and flat. No figures.
