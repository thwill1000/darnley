# LOC021_STABLES

Pixel art, VGA 256-colour palette, 480x320 pixels (displayed at 240x160), style of early 1990s point-and-click adventure games such as Police Quest or King's Quest VI. Hard pixel edges throughout, no anti-aliasing, no modern rendering techniques. Muted period-appropriate colour palette. Static background scene, no characters, no UI elements, no text overlays.

Stable block complex with mixed conditions. The focal point is a two-part structure: one side shows traditional stable doors and windows with loose slates visible on the roof (rendered as dark irregular shapes), the other side converted to a garage with a large Daimler barely visible beneath a worn tarpaulin (suggested as pale draped form). The stable yard between buildings is rendered with snow and scattered straw mixed with dark patches of engine oil spilling. A loose box shows a bay horse's head briefly visible through an opening—rendered as a simple warm brown shape. Tack room door is suggested. Walls are weathered brick in muted brown-reds; paint flakes are suggested through texture. Colour palette: weathered brick reds, dark roof slates, pale tarpaulin, brown wood doors, pale snow, dark oil stains. Cold, slightly dingy feeling. No figures.
